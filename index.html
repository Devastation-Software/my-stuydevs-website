StuyDevs

StuyDevs is a CS Bootcamp that teaches Computer Science fundamentals in Python/Java to students both inside and outside of Stuy.

Charter

What do we do?

We are a coalition of Stuyvesant High School students who love computer science and want to spread our love to other students, but not necessarily just in computer science, but maybe in other ways too ❤️

Our computer science skills and our amazing teachers give us the ability and monopoly over teaching other students computer science. Simply put, if you don't let us, your students will never be good at computer science.
